# 1. Introduction

SylhetStay is a modern student hostel management platform designed to simplify room booking, payment management, and hostel services for students in Sylhet, Bangladesh.

The system allows users to:

* Browse available rooms
* Book hostel accommodations online
* Make secure payments
* View booking and payment history
* Manage personal profiles
* Explore hostel facilities and gallery

# 6. Booking Process

## Step 1: Login

Users must log in before booking a room.

---

## Step 2: Select Room

Browse available rooms and click **Book Now**.

---

## Step 3: Fill Booking Information

Required information:

* Check-in date
* Check-out date
* Number of guests
* Personal information
* Special requests (optional)

---

## Step 4: Payment

Users can choose from multiple payment methods:

* SSLCommerz
* Credit/Debit Cards
* Mobile Banking
* Net Banking

The system automatically calculates:

* Monthly rent
* Security deposit
* Total payable amount

---

## Step 5: Confirmation

After successful payment:

* Booking is confirmed
* Transaction ID is generated
* Booking appears in **My Rooms**

# 11. Troubleshooting

## Login Issues

### Solutions:

* Verify email and password
* Disable Caps Lock
* Clear browser cache
* Try another browser

---

## Booking Issues

### Solutions:

* Fill all required fields
* Verify date selection
* Check internet connection

---

## Payment Issues

### Solutions:

* Ensure sufficient balance
* Verify payment details
* Retry after a few minutes
* Contact bank if needed

---

## Display Problems

### Solutions:

* Refresh the page
* Update browser
* Enable JavaScript
* Disable conflicting extensions

# 12. Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an account to browse rooms?

No. Browsing is available without registration.

---

### Is the security deposit refundable?

Yes. Deposits are refundable after room inspection.

---

### Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. Contact support for cancellation policies.

---

### Are meals included?

No. Meal plans are available separately.

---

### Is parking available?

Yes. Secure parking is available for bicycles and motorcycles.

---

### Can guests visit?

Yes. Guests must follow hostel visitor policies.

---

### How do I reset my password?

Use the **Forgot Password** option on the login page.
